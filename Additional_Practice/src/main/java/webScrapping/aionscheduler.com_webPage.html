AION is designed by hospice & palliative homecare agency for efficient management of their on the road, in-office staff for telehealth and for in clinic patient visits and features

  • Efficient routing of both Hospice and Palliative care staff
  • Support for Work zones, practitioner/patient familiarity, skills matching, travel time etc.
  • Immediate & same day appointments
  • First, emergent and regular patient visits.
  • Electronic Visit Verification.
  • Real-time notification of new and cancelled appointments.

Electronic Visit Verification (EVV)

EVV is built-in in the AION Scheduler and keeps of all information required by EVV when encounters used for billing are created.

Patient Health

AION presents patient health information on the AION app enabling MCW to focus on service delivery. The health information is pulled from the customer’s EMR. Health information includes vitals, medication, and documentation.

Manage Clinical Notes

AION enables voice based notes which are transcribed from voice to text using an AI engine and is stored directly into the EMR.

Manage Visit Frequency

MCWs use AION to manage patient’s visit frequency or to schedule a next visit date.
